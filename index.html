About Us

Our Origin, Values, and Commitment to Quality

Syntax Syndicate Cafe came to be when two bright, like-minded individuals, John Charles Goodwin and Toyota Taladro, came together to brainstorm a business idea. Here at Syntax Syndicate Cafe, we firmly believe that the customer is always right, and we always make sure to meet their expectations.


Meet the Team Behind the Drinks

Toyota Taladro

Toyota Taladro, the very passionate owner of Syntax Syndicate Cafe, boasts a rich background of culinary upbringing. He graduated as Magna Summa Winta Laude at Italy's top patissier school. Despite being gilded with tons of achievements, Toyota manages to stay humble amidst it all.

John Charles Goodwin

John Charles Goodwin, who is an ex-convict of the Alcatraz Prison, reformed his ways to become a world renowned coffee barista. Hailing from the United States, Charles knows the value and simplicity of a good coffee, and wishes to bring it to people who haven't had such an experience yet.



Our Cafe's Mission

Our mission here at Syntax Syndicate Cafe is to be able to serve anyone from anywhere a good cup of coffee, along with some delicious pastries to go with it.
