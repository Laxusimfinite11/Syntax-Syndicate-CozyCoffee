Our Menu

Coffee


  • Espresso

    • A strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

  • Americano

    • Espresso diluted with hot water, resulting in a milder flavor.

  • Latte

    • Espresso combined with steamed milk and topped with a small amount of foam.

  • Cappuccino

    • Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

  • Macchiato

    • Espresso with a small amount of milk or foam.


Pastry


  • Croissant

    • A buttery, flaky pastry with a crescent shape, made from a dough that's layered with butter and rolled to create a light, airy texture.

  • Danish

    • A sweet, multilayered pastry that can be filled with fruit, custard, or cheese. It has a crisp, flaky texture and is often glazed with sugar.

  • Muffin

    • A soft, dome-shaped baked good that can be sweet or savory, typically made with ingredients like berries, nuts, or chocolate chips.

  • Scone

    • A lightly sweetened, crumbly pastry, often served with clotted cream and jam. It can be flavored with ingredients like fruit, cheese, or herbs.

  • Éclair

    • A delicate pastry made from choux dough, filled with cream (like vanilla or chocolate) and topped with a shiny glaze. It has a light and airy texture.

About Us

CozyCup Café is a warm and inviting space where the comfort of home meets the charm of a local coffee shop. Nestled in a quiet corner of the community, CozyCup Café offers a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxing with a book, catching up with friends, or finding inspiration for your next creative project. Our menu features a variety of specialty coffees, teas, and freshly baked goods, all made with the finest ingredients to ensure every sip and bite is delightful. Whether you’re stopping by for your morning brew or settling in for a cozy afternoon, CozyCup Café is the perfect place to unwind and savor the simple pleasures of life.
